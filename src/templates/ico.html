About Leonardo.

Leonardo’s blockchain-based supercompute protocol provides customers with the fastest, simplest, and most affordable cloud-based rendering solution on the market.

Using Leonardo’s desktop application, artists, designers, manufacturers, and corporations of every size and scale will be able to purchase the compute power needed to complete a render, without ever sending their work to a service provider. This system offers both an unparalleled user experience and a high level of intellectual property security.

In addition to operating as a method of transaction, the LEOS token will give individuals access to render miners. This way - although providers of Leonardo compute power will be limited to large-scale GPU aggregators - individuals will be able to share in the reward of render mining by using LEOS tokens to gain access to miners in GPU hosting facilities.

Problem Definition.

Slow Process

Using the current market leaders in the cloud-based rendering space, it can take up to 24 hours to render a single image, and up to 1 month to render 1 second of animation.

IP Theft

All current cloud-based rendering solutions require work to be either uploaded or accessed remotely, leaving billions of dollars of intellectual property susceptible to theft by hackers.

Hardware Costs

Multiple GPU’s are prohibitively expensive for the average consumer - a single GPU can cost up to $1,200.00 USD – and requires an immense amount of electricity.

Work Flow

Creators want to work using their own tools, but all current cloud render solutions force creators to work in remote, creating problematic conditions for CG operators.

Our Mission.

Just about every industry on earth uses rendering in some part of their supply chain. Aerospace, healthcare, and manufacturing all procure rendering services at some point in their operation cycle, and while we’re all visual effects geeks who would love to make the production of sci-fi space epics cost-effective for indie filmmakers, we would also like to pass on lower costs of just about everything to consumers.

Leonardo is not just rendering for the people; it’s value to the people!
