Our Team

We’re a team of makers that weren’t satisfied with the rendering services we were using. We created Leonardo to simplify the process by taking rendering completely off our plate and leaving it in the cloud.

Our team’s past experience with blockchain started some interesting conversations about how rendering could be reimagined if we leveraged the world’s computing power.

And thus, Leonardo was born.
